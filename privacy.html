Privacy Policy

Tempo Controller (Video Speed Controller) is a Chrome extension that allows users to control video playback speed directly in the browser.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All operations happen locally in your browser, ensuring your privacy is maintained.

Permissions

  • activeTab: Control video playback only after you interact with the extension popup.
  • scripting: Inject scripts into the active tab only after your action.
  • storage: Save your preferred playback speed locally in your browser.

Third-party Services

This extension does not use any third-party services, tracking, or analytics. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Usage

All processing is done locally. No information about your browsing, videos, or activity is transmitted over the internet.

Contact

If you have questions or concerns regarding this privacy policy, please contact the developer through the GitHub repository.
