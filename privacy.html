Privacy Policy

Tempo Controller (Video Speed Controller) is a Chrome extension that allows users to control and adjust the playback speed of HTML5 videos directly on web pages.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. All functionality runs locally in your browser, ensuring full privacy.

Permissions

  • storage: Saves user preferences such as selected playback speed, toggle state, and floating badge position. All data is stored locally and never transmitted.
  • Host permissions (http://*/*, https://*/*): Needed to detect and control HTML5 video elements on visited web pages. No website content or browsing data is collected or stored.

Third-party Services

No third-party services, analytics, or tracking are used. Everything runs locally in your browser.

Usage

All processing happens locally. No browsing history, personal activity, or video content is transmitted over the internet.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, contact the developer via the GitHub repository.
